Admissions

Welcome to the admissions page of Golden Tots School. We are excited to have you join our school community. Below, you’ll find all the information you need to enroll your child.

Enrollment Process

To enroll your child at Golden Tots School, follow these steps:

  1. Fill out the Admission Form.
  2. Submit the completed form along with the required documents to the school office.
  3. Attend an interview with the admissions team (if required).
  4. Pay the enrollment fees to secure your child’s spot.

Requirements

Please ensure you have the following documents ready:

  • Birth certificate of the child.
  • Passport-sized photos of the child.
  • Previous school reports (if applicable).
  • Immunization records.

Fee Structure

Our fee structure is as follows:

Program Annual Fees (KES)
Playgroup 50,000
PP1 and PP2 60,000
Grade 1 - Grade 6 70,000
